### Adobo (Chicken/Pork)

**Ingredients:**
- Chicken/pork
- Soy sauce
- Vinegar
- Garlic
- Bay leaves
- Peppercorns

**Steps:**
1. Marinate meat in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
2. Simmer until tender.
3. Reduce sauce for flavor.
